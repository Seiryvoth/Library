អិចស្ប៉ូណង់ស្យែល​​ លោការីត
\(\dfrac{a^m}{a^n}=a^{m-n}\) \(\log (a\times b)=\log a+\log b\)
\(a^m\times a^n=a^{m+n}) \(\log\dfrac{a}{b}=\log a-\log b\)
\((a\times b)^n=a^nb^n\) \(\log a^n=n\log a\)
\((a^m)^n=a^{m\times n}\) \(\log_b a=\log_b x\times \log_x a\)
\(a^{\frac{1}{n}}=\sqrt[n]{a}\) \(\log_b a=\dfrac{\log_x a}{\log_x b\)
\(\left(\dfrac{a}{b}\right)^n=\dfrac{a^n}{b^n},(b\neq 0)\) \(\log_a 1=0\)
\(a^0=1\) \( \log_a a=1\)
\(a^{-n}=\dfrac{1}{a^n},(a\neq 0)\) \(\log_e a=\ln a\)
\(a^{\frac{m}{n}}=\sqrt[n]{a^m\) \(\ln e=1\)

រូបមន្តបន្ថែម

\[\sin^2\alpha+\cos^2\alpha=1\]
\[\tan\alpha.\cot\alpha=1 \]
\[\tan\alpha=\frac{\sin\alpha}{\cos\alpha}=\frac{1}{\cot\alpha} \]
\[\cot\alpha=\frac{\cos\alpha}{\sin\alpha}=\frac{1}{\tan\alpha}\]
\[1+\tan^2\alpha=\frac{1}{\cos^2\alpha}=\sec^2\alpha \]
\[1+\cot^2\alpha=\frac{1}{\sin^2\alpha}\]
\[\sin2\alpha=2\sin\alpha\cos\alpha\]
\[\cos2\alpha=2\cos^2\alpha-1\]
\[\tan2\alpha=\frac{2\tan\alpha}{1-\tan^2\alpha} \]
\[\cot2\alpha=\frac{\cot^2\alpha-1}{2\cot\alpha} \]
