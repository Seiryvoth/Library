រូបមន្តសង្ខេប

\(\lim\limits_{x\to 0}\dfrac{\sin x}{x}=1\) \(\lim\limits_{x\to 0}\dfrac{\sin ax}{bx}=\dfrac{a}{b}\)
\(\lim\limits_{x\to 0}\dfrac{\tan x}{x}=1\) \(\lim\limits_{x\to 0}\dfrac{\tan ax}{bx}=\dfrac{a}{b}\)
\(\lim\limits_{x\to 0}\dfrac{1-\cos x}{x}=0\) \(\lim\limits_{x\to 0}\dfrac{1-\cos ax}{ax}=0\)
\(\lim\limits_{x\to 0}\dfrac{1-\cos x}{x^2}=\dfrac{1}{2}\) \(\lim\limits_{x\to 0}\dfrac{1-\cos ax}{(bx)^2}=\dfrac{1}{2}\left(\dfrac{a}{b}\right)^2\)

រូបមន្តសង្ខេប

\(\lim\limits_{x\to +\infty}e^x=+\infty\) \(\lim\limits_{x\to -\infty}e^x=0\)
\(\lim\limits_{x\to +\infty}\dfrac{x}{e^x}=0\) \(\lim\limits_{x\to +\infty}\dfrac{x^n}{e^x}=0,(n>0)\)
\(\lim\limits_{x\to +\infty}\dfrac{e^x}{x}=+\infty\) \(\lim\limits_{x\to +\infty}\dfrac{e^x}{x^n}=+\infty,(n>0)\)
\(\lim\limits_{x\to 0}\dfrac{e^x-1}{x}=1\) \(\lim\limits_{x\to 0}\dfrac{e^{ax}-1}{ax}=1\)
\(\lim\limits_{x\to 0}(1+x)^{\frac{1}{x}}=e\) \(\lim\limits_{x\to 0}(1+ax)^{\frac{1}{ax}}=e\)
\(\lim\limits_{x\to \pm\infty}\left(1+\dfrac{1}{x}\right)^x=e\) \(\lim\limits_{x\to \pm\infty}\left(1+\dfrac{1}{ax}\right)^{ax}=e\)

រូបមន្តសង្ខេប

\(\lim\limits_{x\to 0^+}\ln x=-\infty\) \(\lim\limits_{x\to +\infty}\ln x=+\infty\)
\(\lim\limits_{x\to +\infty}\dfrac{\ln x}{x}=0\) \(\lim\limits_{x\to +\infty}\dfrac{\ln x}{x^n}=0,(n>0)\)
\(\lim\limits_{x\to 0^+}x\ln x=0\) \(\lim\limits_{x\to +\infty}x^n\ln x=0,(n>0)\)
